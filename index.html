The Flickering Planet

How NASA's Black Marble reveals the changing rhythm of cities after dark

Every night, satellites record the artificial light emitted from Earth's surface. NASA's Black Marble dataset combines these observations into monthly maps of nighttime illumination, providing a consistent record of urban lighting over time. Explore twelve years of observations from Berlin and Kyiv to discover how seasons, technology and major events have shaped the urban night.
